I have been a Library Assistant at the Philadelphia Museum of Art for the past five years, and am currently enrolled in the MLIS program at Drexel University’s College of Computing and Informatics with a concentration in both Archival Studies and Digital Libraries. I have been able to take courses in RDA cataloging, LC classification, collection care, special collections librarianship.
My current position has evolved to include collection development and care, curating library installations, and helping at donor events in which fragile and rare materials are on view.
I would like to further develop my professional skills overall, and in particular in the areas of digitization and rare materials.
