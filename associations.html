I am a member of the following organizations:

  • The Art Libraries Society of North America is a group of professionals in the field of art and design librarianship and image management.
  • Philadelphia Area Consortium of Special Collections Libraries is a group of member libraries in the Philadelphia region. The consortium collaborates on projects, shares skills, and identifies shared priorities.
  • The Rare Books and Manuscripts Section of the Association of College and Research Libraries (ACRL) works to promote the understanding of information management with respect to rare books, manuscripts, and other types of special collections.
  • The Association of College & Research Libraries is a division of the American Library Association (ALA). ACRL develops programs and services to enhance the skills of academic and research librarians.
